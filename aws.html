Cloud Infrastructure Plan

First, we define our goals for the user experience. Then, we compare two leading cloud platforms to see how they can help us achieve those goals and at what cost.

Our Goals for the User Experience

High Performance

The application must be fast and responsive, even during peak hours.

Scalable & Ready for Growth

The infrastructure must handle a growing user base and sudden traffic spikes without crashing.

Secure & Reliable

User data must be protected, and the application should be available and online at all times.
